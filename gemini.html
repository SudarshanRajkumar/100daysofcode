The Evolution of Google AI

A visual timeline showcasing key milestones and breakthroughs that led to the development of Gemini.

2011

The Google Brain Project

The Google Brain project is founded, marking the beginning of the company's dedicated, large-scale deep learning research.

2014

DeepMind Acquisition

Google acquires DeepMind, a leading AI research company, which becomes a key contributor to future AI models and systems.

2016

AlphaGo's Victory

DeepMind's AlphaGo program defeats the world champion Go player, a major milestone demonstrating AI's ability to master complex strategic games.

2017

The Transformer Architecture

Google researchers publish a paper on the Transformer architecture, a revolutionary neural network that becomes the foundation for modern large language models.

2023

Bard is Launched

As a response to the growing interest in conversational AI, Google launches Bard, a chatbot initially powered by its LaMDA model.

2023

Google DeepMind Unification

Google merges the Google Brain team and DeepMind into a single, unified research lab to accelerate its AI development.

2023

Introducing Gemini 1.0

Google announces Gemini 1.0, its most capable and multimodal AI model family, with versions tailored for different tasks and devices.

2024

The Gemini Rebrand

Bard is officially rebranded as "Gemini" to reflect its new foundation on the more advanced Gemini models.

2024

Gemini Live & Project Astra

Google introduces new projects and features, including Gemini Live and the universal AI assistant prototype, Project Astra.
